- Comparison between traditional Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD) and Weight Based Training.

### Description Summary
The video explores the transition from traditional gradient-based optimization techniques, specifically Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD), to newer Weight Based Training approaches. It examines the mechanisms of how Weight Based Training operates, its potential advantages in modern research, and the reasons why it is being explored as a viable alternative to SGD for future AI model training. The discussion focuses on the implications for neural network architectures and Large Language Models (LLMs).
